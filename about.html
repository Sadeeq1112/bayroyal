ABOUT BAYROYAL INTERNATIONAL LIMITED

BAY ROYAL INTERNATIONAL LIMITED is one of the Companies in Nigeria that is
involved in the rapid development of the Nigerian economy in line with the
Government on Civil engineering works.

Our Vision

Our vision is to stimulate increasing and sustainable private sector investment, generating substantial interest in the building environment and infrastructure development. We aim to create a private sector-led growth and development in diverse areas of the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to achieve effective collaboration between the public and private sectors, fostering a symbiotic relationship that leads to the rapid development of the Nigerian economy in line with the Government on Civil engineering works.

Company History

BAY ROYAL INTERNATIONAL LIMITED is a company registered and established in the year 2006 in accordance with the Companies and Allied matters Act (CAMA). It has over the years established itself in the civil/electrical business environment as well as carved a niche in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) arena.

As an engineering firm, it is made up of professionals with vast and wide experience in their various chosen fields. BAY ROYAL INTERNATIONAL LIMITED has professionals such as:

  • Builders
  • Water Engineers
  • Civil/Structural Engineers
  • Electro-Mech. Engrs
  • Consultants
  • Architects
  • Land Surveyors
  • Estate Surveyors
  • Topographers/Cartographers
  • Environmental Technicians/Engineers
